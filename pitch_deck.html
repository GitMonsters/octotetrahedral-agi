🔗 The Chain Mapping Rule

Step 1: Find color-8 connected components (structural markers)

Step 2: Trace 4-connected chains outward through colored cells

Step 3: Chain defines color mapping: farthest → nearest

Step 4: Remove all 8s and chain cells from grid

Step 5: Apply compound color mappings (2→4→1, 5→7)

Step 6: Expand: stamp 8-group shape at each mapped source
